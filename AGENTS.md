# SheSafe — AI Agent Instructions

## Product Identity

SheSafe is a public-interest safety tool. It provides civic infrastructure for safety reporting, not a commercial or social platform.

## Rules

1. **Guest-first.** Do not require authentication for core functionality.
2. **Privacy.** Do not expose reporter identities or location data without clear consent.
3. **No gamification.** Safety is serious. No streaks, badges, or engagement mechanics.
4. **Public interest.** All design decisions should prioritize community safety over growth metrics.
5. **Branch naming:** \`feat/*\`, \`fix/*\`, \`docs/*\`, \`refactor/*\`, \`chore/*\`.

## Ecosystem Standards

All ecosystem repos follow: https://github.com/sparshsam/ecosystem-standards
