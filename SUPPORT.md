# Support

SheSafe is an early-stage safety mapping concept. Support is best-effort and focused on reproducible bugs, privacy concerns, documentation, and careful product direction.

## Before Opening An Issue

- Check the [README](README.md), [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), and [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
- Search existing issues and pull requests.
- Do not share private reports, personal addresses, exact personal movement histories, credentials, or sensitive safety details.

## Good Issue Reports

- Describe the map, pin, moderation, or interface behavior involved.
- Use synthetic examples when discussing location or safety data.
- Include browser and device details for UI issues.

For vulnerabilities or sensitive reports, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
